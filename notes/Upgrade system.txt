Upgrade System

There will be three types of upgrades:
Permanent (stats that last the whole game)
- Includes: MaxHp, points, lootRange, lootTime

Temporary (status that last until a map change)
- Includes: lootRank, shot accuracy, explosive damage, bullet damage, player speed
